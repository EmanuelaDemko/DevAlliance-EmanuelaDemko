What is My Everywhere Library?

My Everywhere Library is a simple, personal book tracking application that helps you organize your reading life. Whether you are an avid reader or just getting started, this tool lets you keep track of what you have read, what you are currently reading, and what is on your wish list.

Built as a school project for DevAlliance Training, this application demonstrates the use of modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap 5, and DataTables. All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage, so your library is always available.

Features

Add Books

Easily add books to your library with details like title, author, status, rating, and personal notes.

Track Status

Organize books by status: To Be Read (TBR), Currently Reading, Read, or Did Not Finish (DNF).

Filter and Search

Quickly find books using the search bar or filter by reading status with one click.

Rate Books

Give each book a rating from 1 to 5 stars to remember how much you enjoyed it.

Dark Mode

Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable reading day or night.

Responsive Design

Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices for reading on the go.

How to Use

1
Go to My Books

Navigate to the "My Books" page from the navigation bar to see your full library.

2
Add a New Book

Click the "Add Book" button and fill in the details: title, author, reading status, rating, and any notes.

3
Manage Your Collection

Use the edit and delete buttons to update your books as you progress through them. Change the status when you start or finish reading.

4
Filter and Search

Use the filter buttons (All, TBR, Reading, Read, DNF) or the search bar to quickly find specific books.

5
Check Your Dashboard

Visit the Home page to see your reading statistics at a glance, including total books and status counts.
